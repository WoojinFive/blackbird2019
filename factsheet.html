Factsheet for Registered Employers

Hours

Booth hours are from 10:00 am to 3:30 pm. Representatives may arrive after 8:00 am on the day of the event to set up.

Twitter

Our twitter hashtag is #P4Efair.

Meals

A full breakfast is available between 8:30 am and 10:30 am and a buffet lunch is available between 12:00 pm and 2:00 pm. Meals will be served in the Employer Lounge. Bringing your own reusable water bottle will help us reduce waste at the event.

Shipping

Employer shipments may arrive up to two days prior to the Fair but items may not remain in the facility beyond 4:30 pm on the day of the Fair. For further details regarding shipping materials, please refer to our Shipping and Handling Procedures.

Parking

Parking is free. There will be staff available in the morning to assist you with unloading materials, please follow signs to the loading dock. Additional overflow lots are available should the main lot be full; a shuttle bus will run on a continuous loop from 10 am to 4 pm.

Printing

Please bring a sufficient supply of recruitment material as no printing or photocopying facilities are available on site.

Identification

Each representative will be provided with a wristband to allow access to the employer lounge and reentry to the fair. Exhibitor identification badges are available if needed. Badges are not personalized but will accommodate business card insertion.

Wi-Fi

We will provide a Wi-Fi password at the event for use in the exhibit hall, public Wi-Fi will be available in other areas of the building. Please be aware that the Wi-Fi may not support all streaming activities. If you plan to show a video or presentation, we recommend bringing it in an alternate format as well.

Electrical

Each booth will be equipped with one electrical outlet. If you require multiple outlets, please bring a power bar. Exhibitors are responsible for providing their own extension cords. Any electrical equipment you are using must be in compliance with the Ontario Electrical Safety Code and may be inspected on site. Audio-visual Equipment Participating organizations are responsible for supplying their own equipment. Audio-visual equipment, booth accessories, and exhibit systems may be rented by contacting Hahn Rentals at 519.746.8955 or 1.800.563.4246. A list of services is available at www.hahnrentals.com. Hahn Rentals will invoice organizations directly for any additional rentals.

Booths and Displays

Booth displays and equipment including all signs, banners, furniture, etc. may not extend into the aisles. All demonstrations must be contained within the booth. Any side panel displays must not exceed 5 feet in height. Any structures that exceed 12 feet, including signs, lighting, displays, balloons, etc. must be approved by Partnerships for Employment. Partnerships for Employment reserves the right to restrict sounds from any source that may interfere with activities in neighbouring booths. Exhibitors may not distribute any literature or promotional items for their organization or any other organization beyond their exhibit space. Exhibitors may not sell or promote any products or services at the event.
