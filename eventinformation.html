Just us for the IT Job Fair!

The largest college wide recruitment fair is here! With many organizations attending, you will find a wide variety of career opportunities and graduate programs.

The ITJob Fair is open to all NSCC students and alumni to discover amazing opportunities, and network with potential future employers, professional associations and programs.

Why You Should Attend

  • To network with potential employers
  • To investigate and research career options
  • To obtain information from employers on
  • Career opportunities
  • Educational requirements
  • Corporate cultures
  • Job requirements
  • Industry growth
  • Industry trends
  • Skills and qualifications
