Exchanging and Networking

The IT Job Fair offers direct contact between companies / institutions and students of NSCC. At the IT Job Fair 2019, 24 exhibitors presented information about job opportunities such as internships, co-op programs, entry level positions and research projects.

Company Presentations

Participating companies and institutions have the additional opportunity to exclusively present their business to a relevant student audience. Company presentations allow for employer branding as well as for the dispersion of information on current programs and projects.

Workshops and Career Talks

The IT Job Fair program consists of various workshops and sessions on career related topics. Experienced experts provide first-hand insight and answer questions about application strategies, entrepreneurship and labor market needs.

The Annual IT Job Fair

Worldwide enterprises exhibit on the NSCC IT Campus along with regional companies and freshly founded start-ups. The annual IT Job Fair is facilitating contacts between NSCC students and potential employers from the various range of areas.

Employers

The list of employers includes large international companies such as IBM, Lockheed Martin Canada, CGI as well as regional firms, like REDspace, Maplewave, Sobeys, Nova Scotia Power/ Emera and others.

The popular networking platform

The IT Job Fair has grown into a popular networking platform for exhibitors, students, and numerous alumni. Companies are invited to give presentations and meet young talents for general information or application interviews.
