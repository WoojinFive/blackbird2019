FAQ FOR STUDENTS

1. Why should I attend the job fair?

The job fair targets IT students of all fields. It gives you an opportunity to network with industry professionals, and to present yourself as a valuable asset to an organization.

2. What does the job fair offer for visitors?

The job fair consists of a variety of opportunities. These include internships, co-ops, work-terms, and full time entry-level positions.

3. Where can I find more information about the job fair?

You can find more information about our job fair through our website. Should you wish to inquire any further, feel free to email or call us.

4. Is it mandatory to register for the job fair? When will registration take place?

No. Registration for the job fair is not mandatory; however it is strongly recommended. Registration will take place roughly a month before the event. Students will be notified ahead of time to fill out a form containing all relevant information.

5. Is there a fee to pay?

The job fair is free of charge for all students.

6. What is the dress code?

Business casual is preferred.

7. What do companies expect from students?

Companies are in search for qualified employees of their respective fields. They are in search for individuals who are able to collaborate individually and with a group.

8. Do I have to bring any documents (ie: resume)?

It is recommended to bring your resume, a cover letter and/or a business card. This ensures the greatest chance of success. The more prepared you are, the better!

9. Do job interviews take place at the job fair?

Depending on the company, some interviews will be conducted in what we call a “on-the-spot interview”. Be prepared!
