HOW TO PREPARE

There are a lot of things that you can do to prepare for meeting a potential employer. Here are a few suggestions. Before you go to the fair, make sure that you:

1. Research companies

Decide on employers whom you want to target when at the fair and prepare some tailored questions that spark conversation.

2. Prepare your resume/portfolio

Prepare a short, good structured resume, not more than 1-2 pages. Furthemore, you can create your portfolio using Github and add this link to your resume.


Tip: create your professional resume by using support provided by NSCC Career Services or attend workshops before the IT Job Fair.

3. Professional Dress

Business professional attire is recommended for all students attending the Job fair. Business casual is recommended at a minimum.

4. Check your attitude

Exude enthusiasm and self-confidence, stay positive during the IT Job Fair!

5. Practice your introduction

Prepare an elevator speech: An elevator pitch, elevator speech, or elevator statement is a short summary used to quickly and simply define who you are as well as your abilities and strengths.

6. Prepare yourself for contact with the employers

Stand out with smart questions, be thoughtful, and listen well. Show that you have done some research, what you know and ask for more, keep the focus on the job and the company.

7. Bring resumes/business cards

8. Follow up after the event

Follow up with companies with companies you have contacted. Furthermore, extend the connection beyond the IT Job Fair by asking for business cards or creating LinkedIn connections.
